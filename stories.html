Positive Good News Stories

True, life-affirming stories of hope, kindness, and humanity. #PassItOn

Changing Course in Life

Peter Pretorius found himself stranded in Mozambique during a severe famine. Witnessing the suffering firsthand, he was deeply moved by the dire need around him. Instead of escaping back to safety, he chose to stay and help. That experience became a turning point in his life.

The Language of Love

At just 18, Mia Lee launched a nonprofit to teach language skills to refugees across the globe. Moved by stories of displacement and communication barriers, she saw language as a bridge to opportunity and belonging. Her organization now helps thousands find their voice in new lands.

Living on a Prayer

Jon Bon Jovi is more than a rockstar; he’s a passionate advocate for hunger relief. At his JBJ Soul Kitchen, he rolls up his sleeves to help feed communities in need, offering meals to those who can’t afford them. The restaurant operates on a pay-what-you-can basis, showing that kindness and community can go hand in hand.

Classroom Hero

A struggling student was on the brink of giving up when a teacher noticed his potential and offered a kind word of encouragement. She took the time to listen and believe in him when no one else did. Her unwavering support inspired him to turn his life around, and he went on to achieve great success, forever grateful for her belief in him./p>

Bridging Generations

Teens and seniors came together through a storytelling program, where they shared experiences and life lessons. The bond they formed proved that age is just a number, as both generations learned from each other.

Paws of Hope

An animal shelter worker dedicated herself to saving abandoned pets, going above and beyond to care for them. She personally fostered many animals, providing them with the love and attention they needed to heal.

Unexpected Hero

During a devastating flood, a young volunteer sprang into action, helping evacuate a stranded neighborhood. With courage and determination, they guided families to safety, earning the admiration of everyone around. Their quick thinking and selflessness made them an unexpected hero in the crisis.

Growing Together

A group of neighbors came together to transform a vacant lot into a vibrant community garden. Through teamwork and shared effort, they cultivated not only plants but also a stronger sense of unity. The garden became a symbol of growth, where friendships blossomed alongside vegetables.
